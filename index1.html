THE STORY OF SOLARCITY

SolarCity was founded in July 2006 by brothers Peter and Lyndon Rive, based on a suggestion for a solar company concept by their cousin, Elon Musk, who is the chairman of the company and also the CEO of electric-car maker Tesla Motors (TSLA). SolarCity is America's largest residential solar power provider with over 13000 employess, operating in 19 states with 75 operation centers. Since 2008, the company has received financing from Morgan Stanley, USbancorp, PG&E Corporation, Citi Bank and Google. SolarCity's customers include tens of thousands of homeowners, more than 400 schools including Stanford University, government agencies such as the U.S. Armed Forces and Department of Homeland Security, and well-known corporate clients, including eBay, HP, Intel, Walgreens and Walmart.
